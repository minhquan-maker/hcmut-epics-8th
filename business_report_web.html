Service Lifecycle
Phase 1
Pre-Flood
Early warning & preparedness
  • Location-based flood alert system with real-time push notifications
  • Risk assessment & vulnerability mapping for target zones
  • Evacuation route planning with safe shelter coordination
  • Community preparedness education & safety protocol delivery
  • Weather data integration from meteorological agencies
Phase 2
In-Flood
Rescue coordination & emergency response
  • SOS emergency signal — victims request immediate rescue assistance
  • Real-time victim↔rescuer matching with live GPS tracking
  • Rescuer team dispatch with Leader/Co-leader coordination
  • On-ground incident reporting from citizens for situational awareness
  • Live flood map with dynamic danger zone visualization
Phase 3
Post-Flood
Recovery, analysis & resource distribution
  • Damage assessment & impact data analysis from collected reports
  • Resource allocation & distribution optimization across affected zones
  • Recovery coordination between authorities, NGOs, and communities
  • Historical flood data archive for future preparedness modeling
  • Structured datasets for government planning & policy reporting
